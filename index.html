Research

Applications

Pharmacological challenges, healthy ageing, disease models, drug discovery endpoints. The brain changes may be subtle, so reliable detection requires methods you can trust. Each project receives a bespoke analysis for the questions at hand.

Methods & Pipelines

Analysis pipelines in research are rarely well-documented where it matters. I build and contribute to open-source tools to make rigorous analysis more accessible and trustworthy. The goal is lowering barriers to entry for science everyone can build on.
