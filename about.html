Eilidh MacNicol

I am in the final year of my MRC-funded PhD programme at King's College London.
I intend to characterise the changes in structural and functional brain networks which occur during healthy ageing.
If we know what network changes to expect in healthy ageing, we can better understand the changes that happen when ageing is not healthy.

I graduated from the University of Glasgow in 2013 with a BSc(Hons) in neuroscience, having taken modules in human biology, psychology, and chemistry.
Following a short sabbatical to travel, I moved to London to pursue an MSc in neuroimaging at King's College London, which I graduated with distinction in 2015.
My PhD has been an excellent opportunity to realise my data skills; I have developed my Bash, Matlab, and Python coding abilities, and gained an enthusiasm for open science and data analysis methods.
